Randomly Placed Draggable Cards

Made with GSAP's Draggable plugin. The cards are randomly placed within the container and can be dragged around. The placement isn't completely random. It is clustered around the center of the container.

At mobile size (<684px), the dragging and random placement is disabled, and the cards are stacked in a column.
